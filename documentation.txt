### Wiring & Adressing
| Devise               | I²C-Adress | TX-Pin | RX-Pin | Voltage |
+----------------------+------------+--------+--------+---------+
| BMP280               |    0x76    |  none  |  none  |    5    |
| BNO-055              |    0x28    |  none  |  none  |    5    |
| NEO-6M GPS           |    none    |   12   |   11   |    5    |

 }
